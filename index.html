Good news, everyone!

The tenth annual Indy Hackers Holiday Social is happening Friday, December 18th, and you're invited! Please join us, starting at 7pm, for a night full of friends both old and the Hackies Awards.

Indy Hackers will be recognizing individuals in the community who are great promotors of inclusion, empowerment and diversity. We'll be giving out the following awards to recognize members of the Indiana tech community: “Community Member”, “Mentor”, “Teacher & Trainer”, “Level Up” and “Rising Star”.

Everyone is welcome - junior devs, senior devs, non-devs who want to know more about programming, Pythonistas, Javanese, COBOLians, Scalanites, PHPers, Rubyists, .NET nuts, Perlsians. We just want to get the tech community in Indianapolis together to play some games and have some fun.

As always, the Holiday Social is completely free!

Agenda:

  • 15 minutes - Gather and say Hello
  • 20 minutes - Hand out the Hackies!
  • Until you’ve had your fill of digital local connectedness... Let’s do what we normally do and hang out!
